Nixie3D was an experiment to see what I could make using CSS 3D transform and how far I could get with simply using CSS.


A Nixie tube, or cold cathode tube, displays numerical or alphabetical characters by applying power to speically shaped cathodes encased within a tube of neon gas and causing them to glow a bright orange color.


I find the look of Nixie tubes extremely fascinating and so I wanted to recreate them using pure CSS.
